Ohio State University to open science park dedicated solely to space research:
• Ohio State University has been chosen by Colorado-based space technology company Voyager Space as the home of its George Washington Carver Science Park; 
• Research topics include water quality, health of animals and crop genetics;
• Operations are expected to start at a couple of locations on campus in 2023, with a permanent structure expected to be built at The Ohio State University Airport by 2025 or 2026; 
•Voyager Space hopes to create 50 full-time jobs in Ohio, generating nearly $6 million in new annual payroll. Salaries for those jobs will exceed $100,000 per year.
