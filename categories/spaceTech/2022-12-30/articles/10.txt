
                                    Japanese startup Space One plans first rocket launch in February -
                        Nikkei Asia
                        
Smaller, low-cost delivery vehicles expected to ignite satellite industry TOKYO/WAKAYAMA, Japan -- Japanese startup Space One will conduct its maiden rocket launch at the end of February, the company said as it unveiled the country's first private-sector launch pad. Space Port Kii is a 15-hectare site in Kushimoto, Wakayama prefecture, south of Osaka that houses a tower for assembling and inspecting rockets as well as a control building.
