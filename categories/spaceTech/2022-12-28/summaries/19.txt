ISRO has imparted nanosatellite assembly courses and relevant training to 31 delegates from 19 nations through UNispace Nanosatellite Assembly & Training by ISRO (UNNATI), conducted by experts and scientists from various ISRO centres. The two-month course included lessons on general satellite technology, nanosatellite design, testing and integration of various subsystems of a nanosatellite. So far 91 participants have benefitted from the three editions of India's UNNATI programme.
