block 1:  Survival of SMEs hinges on technology  - BusinessGhana
Briefing:  
• SMEs must appreciate that markets are becoming more competitive and adapting to Technology is no longer an option. 
• Cybersecurity Tech is necessary in order to protect against attacks from hackers, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic. Necessary precautions should include network security, firewalls, anti-virus applications and computers, data encryption, etc. 
• Automation can simplify all aspects of a business while boosting efficiency and employee productivity. It should be seen as an investment rather than an expenditure. 
• Online payments provide better customer experience with cost reduction in transactions as well as record retention via cloud-hosted data for competitive advantage.
