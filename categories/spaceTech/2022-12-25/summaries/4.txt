block 1: 
• China deployed a mini satellite from its Tiangong (“Heavenly Palace”) space station into low-earth orbit. 
• The 12-kilogram satellite is called the Macao Student Science Satellite 1 and was developed by the China Academy of Space Technology. 
• It is designed to help students in Macao learn about Earth imaging, radio communication and other spaceflight activities according to China Manned Space Agency. 
• The satellite was carried on the Tianzhou 5 cargo spacecraft to the space station and has been catalogued by US Space Force’s 18th Space Defense Squadron. 
• The Tiangong space station consists of three modules: Tianhe ("heavenly river"), Wentian ("quest for heavens") and Mengtian ("dreaming of heaven").
