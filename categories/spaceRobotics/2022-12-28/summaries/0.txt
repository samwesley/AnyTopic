This article highlights the top manufacturing trends from IMTS Chicago, which took place in September 2022. Innovations such as efficient and precise machine tools, machining centers working with cobots, and hybrid machines with 3D printing capabilities were demonstrated at the show, including a new affordable manufacturing solution for making metal parts without dies. Additionally, industrial innovation for space exploration was also showcased at the event with 3D printed habitats for the Moon and mirrors for telescopes. Finally, D:PLOY hardware and software developed by OnRobot simplifies robotics programming to make palletizing easier.
