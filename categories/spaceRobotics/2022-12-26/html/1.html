S.T.E.A.M. Interactive Carts

S.T.E.A.M. Interactive Carts

 

Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex is bringing new educational entertainment to our younger guests! The S.T.E.A.M Team is giving guests the chance to learn more about science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics through fun experiments,

The S.T.E.A.M Team has interactive, pop-up experiences throughout the main visitor complex. During the holiday week of December 26 - 31, 2022, the carts will be in front of the Debus Conference Facility, in front of Gateway: The Deep Space Launch Complex®, and by the Atlantis West Pavilion. Beginning in 2023, they will be in front of Gateway: The Deep Space Launch Complex®, NASA Central, and Constellation Plaza. Each cart will feature different experiments for guests to watch and learn. The hands-on experiments will include:

 

  • Molecules and their different states
  • The center of gravity
  • The vacuum of space
  • Air and air pressure
  • Tricky Science
  • You are a scientist

 

The S.T.E.A.M Team presentations are included with admission.
