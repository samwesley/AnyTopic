Malaysia Coworking Operator Launches Startup-Focused Office Brand

KONGSI WORK, deriving from the Malay word “Kongsi” that translates to “share,” will span 10,000 square feet and offer a variety of workspaces for professionals.

by Aayat Ali

Malaysia-based coworking operator INFINITY8 has launched a new, affordable brand geared towards entrepreneurs, small businesses and startups. 

KONGSI WORK, deriving from the Malay word “Kongsi” that translates to “share,” will span 10,000 square feet and offer a variety of workspaces and amenities for professionals 

Located in Taman Molek, the fully-equipped workspace will feature 51 offices, private meeting rooms, smart-lock doors, automated mailboxes and more. 

“As its name suggests, we envision this brand to be a solution for SMEs and individuals…” said Lee Sheah, founder and CEO of INFINITY8. “We aim to offer a space to nurture local businesses and provide them with a practical option, conducive for growth and community to thrive.”
